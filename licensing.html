Platform Ownership

The PrimeReach platform architecture, codebase, and methodology remain the intellectual property of EVOBRAND Concepts. Agencies license the right to deploy and operate the platform — they do not purchase the underlying software.

Agency License

Agencies Get Full Operational Rights

A PrimeReach license grants the agency full rights to configure, brand, deploy, and operate the platform for their program. Includes all updates, security patches, and support at the licensed tier level.
