The Challenge

Managing Prime–Sub Relationships Shouldn't Be This Hard

Disconnected Workflows

Prime contractors and small subcontractors rely on email threads, spreadsheets, and phone calls to find each other — with no centralized record, visibility, or accountability.

Manual Compliance Burden

DBE, SBE, MBE, and DVBE tracking happens across siloed tools. Audit preparation takes weeks and certification data is frequently out of date when you need it most.

No Real-Time Visibility

Agency staff and district offices have no live view of active opportunities, application status, or which small businesses are engaged — making reporting reactive instead of proactive.

The Platform

Everything Your Agency Needs,
Out of the Box

PrimeReach delivers a complete dual-portal architecture — one for prime contractors, one for small businesses — with the compliance, tooling, and district management built in.

Dual-Portal Architecture

Separate, role-scoped portals for prime contractors and small businesses — each with their own dashboard, profile, and workflow.

DBE / SBE / MBE Certification Support

Built-in program-type filtering, eligibility guidance, and certification tracking for all major small business designations.

Opportunity Management

Primes post structured subcontracting opportunities; small businesses discover, apply, and track status — all in one place.

Real-Time Messaging

In-platform messaging with notification badges keeps primes and subs connected without relying on external email chains.

District-Based Service Hubs

Configurable regional districts allow staff to manage their geographic area independently while agency-wide reporting stays unified.

Analytics & Reporting

Live dashboards for agency staff, prime contractors, and admins — covering engagement, opportunity flow, and compliance metrics.

WCAG 2.1 AA/AAA & Section 508

Accessibility is built in, not bolted on — skip links, ARIA roles, keyboard navigation, and contrast ratios validated against federal standards.

Progressive Web App (PWA)

Works offline, installs to the home screen, and loads instantly — no app store required. Fully mobile-responsive across all device sizes.

White-Label Ready

Every agency-specific value — name, logo, colors, districts, program types, contact info — lives in a single config file. Redeploy in minutes.

Deployment Process

Three Steps to a Live Platform

No long implementation cycles. No custom dev required for standard deployments.

1

Configure

Provide your agency name, logo, color palette, district structure, program types, and contact info. Everything goes into a single config file — no code changes needed.

2

Deploy

We spin up your branded platform on your domain. Database, server, service worker, manifest, and all 45+ pages are pre-configured and accessibility-validated out of the box.

3

Manage

Your staff uses the built-in admin CMS to manage content, users, and announcements. Primes post opportunities. Small businesses apply. You get live reporting dashboards.

Case Study

Deployed and Validated at the State Level

Deployment: Major State DOT — 12 Districts

Statewide Transportation Agency Deployment

PrimeReach was deployed as the official small business enterprise supportive services platform for a major state Department of Transportation, serving all 12 geographic districts. The platform managed dual-portal access for prime contractors and small businesses across DBE, SBE, and MBE program types.

The deployment included a full administrative CMS, real-time messaging, district-based opportunity filtering, analytics dashboards, and a complete PWA implementation — all delivered WCAG 2.1 AA/AAA compliant and Section 508 ready.

"The platform received commendation from the Assistant Deputy Director and the Deputy Director of the Office of Civil Rights for its accessibility compliance, user experience design, and comprehensive feature delivery."
