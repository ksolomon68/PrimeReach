The Process

Four Steps to a Government Contract

No experience with government contracting needed. We walk you through every step.

1

Register Your Skills

Create your free worker profile in under 5 minutes. Select your trade, list your certifications, and tell us where you can work.

2

Get Contract Matches

Our platform instantly maps your trade to active NAICS-coded government contracts using a skills-to-contract matching engine.

3

Complete Vendor Checklist

Work through our guided vendor readiness checklist: SAM.gov registration, capability statement, set-aside education, and more.

4

Connect With Primes

Prime contractors actively searching for your trade category can find your profile and send you a direct connection request.

Fiber installers, cell site technicians, structured cabling specialists, and telecom infrastructure workers are urgently needed across every state. What You Get

Platform Features

Everything you need to go from skilled worker to government contract-ready.

Skills-to-NAICS Matching

Your trade category is automatically mapped to the official government NAICS codes used to award contracts — so you appear in the right searches.

Vendor Readiness Checklist

A guided step-by-step checklist covers SAM.gov registration, entity setup, capability statement creation, and set-aside certification paths.

Prime Contractor Directory

Browse prime contractors actively working government contracts in your trade category and geographic area. Connect directly through the platform.

SAM.gov Opportunity Feed

Live feed of federal contracting opportunities filtered to your NAICS codes so you always know what active contracts match your skill set.

Capability Statement Generator

Build a professional one-page capability statement from your profile. Export as PDF — the standard document prime contractors and agencies require.

Set-Aside Education

Learn about 8(a), HUBZone, SDVOSB, WOSB, and other set-aside programs that can give your business preferential treatment on government bids.
